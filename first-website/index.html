Healthy Eating and Nutrition

Healthy eating is about more than just losing weight; it’s about feeling good and having more energy. A well-balanced diet provides essential nutrients to your body and mind. Learn how to eat right, stay healthy, and improve your quality of life!

Read more on WHO: Healthy Diet

Get started with the healthy food -

  • Plan your healthy meals for the week ahead.
  • Include a variety of food items in every meal.
  • To save the time, Prepare your ingredients in advance.
  • Consider the specific portion sizes to avoid overeating.

Mindful eating is the practice of paying full attention to your food, from how it’s grown to how it tastes. Here are some key practices for mindful eating:

  • Eat slowly and savor every bite.
  • Listen to your body's hunger and fullness cues.
  • Avoid distractions like TV or phones while eating.
  • Choose foods that nourish both your body and mind.
